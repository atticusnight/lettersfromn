About

Cal finds himself on a mountaintop, with nothing but a voice recorder to keep him sane. Following his thoughts and recordings, he comes upon a series of letters-letters from N-and we come upon a dark, convoluted mystery. Told in a series of scripted episodes, this podcast is unlike any other. Produced by Audite Scriptum.
